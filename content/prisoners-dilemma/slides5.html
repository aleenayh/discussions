Scenario 3: The Art Market

Imagine you're going to sell your artwork at a market.

Your friend is considering doing the same thing.

Should you share a booth to showcase your craft?

Or set up your own?

If you combine, you could have a more attractive booth and draw in more customers.

However, it might draw more attention to your friend's crafts than to yours.

If you have your own, you get the full attention and the sales. But it will cost more.

Discuss with your room: What do you choose?
