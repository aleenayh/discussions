On an otherwise normal day, a spaceship enters the Earth's atmosphere and hovers over a major city. Suddenly, a voice is heard from the ship.

"People of Earth: We seek refuge. Our home planet is no longer habitable. Will you allow us to live on this planet?"

Imagine you are the leader of your country. How should you respond?

Option 1: Welcome
Offer the aliens a place to settle and live, while assuring them that Earth's inhabitants will cooperate and share resources.

Option 2: Rejection
Tell the aliens they should look elsewhere; we cannot shelter them.

Option 3: Isolation
Establish an isolated, comfortable zone for them where they can live without much interaction with humans.

Option 4: Offer Assistance, Not Shelter
Politely decline their request, but offer to assist them in finding another suitable planet.

Option 5: Delayed Decision
Request more time to make a decision.

Discuss with your group: what should you do?
What are the risks and benefits of each option?
