Scenario 2. Farming.

A small country relies heavily on farming to support its economy.

However, all the farms need to share irrigation channels so that all farms have an equal share of the water.

In the past, farmers have managed this themselves.

But there are no laws to prevent one farmer from taking more. The country is considering taking over irrigation to make sure it stays fair.

Court is now in session. What will each side say? Try to predict both positions, without making a final judgement.
