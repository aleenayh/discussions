Meet the Team

Here are your hiring options:

Editor

The editor is the leader of the news division, responsible for overseeing all content, making final decisions on what gets published, and ensuring overall quality.

Investigative Journalist

This journalist digs deep into stories, often uncovering hidden truths and scandals. They spend a lot of time researching and interviewing to bring groundbreaking information to the public.

Photojournalist

A key visual storyteller, the photojournalist captures images that bring news stories to life. Their photographs can often tell a story just as powerfully as words.

Fact Checker

Essential for maintaining the newspaper's credibility, the fact checker carefully verifies every detail in a story before it goes to print, ensuring accuracy and truthfulness in reporting.

Digital Editor

Modern media requires an online presence. This person makes sure content is engaging, up-to-date, and optimized for digital platforms like websites and social media.
