Employee Recognition

Zappos, the online shoe retailer, wanted a positive company culture that recognized their best employees.

So, how did they try to solve this problem?

Zappos created a Hero Award.

Employees could recognize each other and give a cape to wear around the office for the day.

The cape served as a visual cue to others that this hero earned recognition from their peers.
