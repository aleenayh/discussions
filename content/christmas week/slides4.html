Imagine you and five of your friends visited the beach for a day.

While your friends swim and play, you take a walk and notice something: the corner of a huge chest, sticking out of the sand.

What should you do with the chest?

1. Share it. Call your friends over and split everything you've found.

2. Hide it. Bury it in the sand and come back to find it later, when your friends aren't around to see – and won't want a share.

3. Donate it. Call a museum or historical society to decide what to do with the chest. Put the money from any sales towards charity.
