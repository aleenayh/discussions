Here are your hiring options:

Game Designer

The designer is the creative mind behind the game's concept, storyline, and mechanics. They envision the game's world and how players interact with it.

Software Developer

This role specializes in writing the code that brings the game to life. They work on implementing the game design in a playable form.

Graphic Artist

The artist makes the visual elements of the game, including 3D character design and environment creation. They bring the game's world and characters to visual reality.

Beta Tester

This role tests the game for bugs, glitches, and gameplay issues. They ensure the game runs smoothly and is enjoyable for players.

Marketer

A marketer promotes the game to the right target audience. This person handles advertising, distribution to stores, and online presence.
