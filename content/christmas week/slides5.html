Imagine you are on the local school board, and a tech company wants you to pilot their latest invention...

Robo-Educators. These robots can teach any subject flawlessly, tailor lessons to individual student needs, and operate 24/7.

But making the switch isn't without risks. With the future of education at stake, which direction do you steer?

1. Switch all classrooms. Replace all human teachers with Robo-Educators for the upcoming school year.

2. Robo-Teaching Assistants. Keep teachers human, but use robots for roles like tutors or lab assistants.

3. Switch some classrooms. Put Robo-Educators in some classrooms, and let parents choose if they want a human or robot teacher for the upcoming school year.

4. Human-AI classrooms. For each classroom with a Robo-Educator, keep a human teacher on staff to supervise and help mentor students.

5. Stay human. Turn the tech company down, and keep your human teachers.

Other school districts are watching, so your choice has an impact on the future of education. What will you choose?
